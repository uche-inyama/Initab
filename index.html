Initab Chrome New Tab Extension For Developers

Replace Chrome's new tab with a dashboard of useful tools to make your job as a programmer easier.

About Initab

Initab is a chrome New Tab Extension for developers that offers a solution to continually having too many tabs open.It also offers several useful tools to make your job as a developer easier with a quick view of your GitHub or GitLab Issue activity, stack Overflow activity, and much more.
